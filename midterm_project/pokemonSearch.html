Want to pick your favorite Pokemon,
But don't know how to start?

You come to the right place!

Rules:

1. Click the button in each Egg Group
2. The computer will randomly select 3 Pokemons from each Egg Group
(Note: The result can be 1 or 2 in special cases)
3. Each Pokemon card has the Pokemon's pokedex, name, picture, and type
4. Each card's color is defined by their primary Pokemon type

There are 15 Egg Groups in total. Enjoy finding Pokemons that interest you!

Rules:

1. After looking through 15 Egg Groups, you can search for Pokemons you are interested in
2. The search result focuses on the Pokemon's characteristic
3-1. Please enter a "Pokedex" in the search bar and click "Search"
3-2. Please re-enter a "Pokedex" if you search an invalid pokedex or the Pokemon doesn't have characteristics
4. The Pokemon card has the Pokemon's name, picture, types, and characteristic
5. The card's color is defined by their primary Pokemon type (the 1st type)

Hope you find your favorite Pokemon in this Pokemon World!
